CrystalsCollector!


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number and you lose the game if your total score goes above it.

The value of each cyrstal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each the game starts, the value of each crystal and random number changes.
